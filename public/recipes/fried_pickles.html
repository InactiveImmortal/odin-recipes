Fried Pickles Recipe

Description

Fried pickles are a popular snack that are easy to make and delicious. They are a great way to use up leftover pickles and make a quick and easy meal.

Ingredients

  • Pickles
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the pickles into strips
  2. Dredge the pickles in flour
  3. Fry the pickles in oil
